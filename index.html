About

Bio

Passionate about exploring the natural world and learning how to best live and grow within this natural environment in conjunction with the advancement of technology. David draws on the art, architecture, culture and people around him, embracing the unique differences and relishing in the many similarities we all share. Colour, line, and shape are the key aspects David explores to create vibrant and loud designs that are clean and sharp whilst maintaining a holistic ethos.

Design / Writing

Mission Control

Initial Launch

First contact will be a brief of understanding. Getting to know your business, the problem requiring a digital solution and the end goal of the potential project. Working closely with your business' point of contact to ensure all criteria is understood and how best a solution can be delivered

Research & Analysis

Thorough research and analysis will commence once first contact has been finalised and approved. Research will consist of collating information into your business, competitors, customers, the problem and the goals of the project. Current trends that align with your business, existing porblems, user persona's and the users journey are integral to this process.

Concept & Design

Concept art and designs will be developed into a design document for close collaboration with your point of contact. This will include evidence from the research, concept art, wireframes, digital mockups and any potential problems that arise from the research.

Collaboration & Refine

During the design process, our team will work closely with your point of contact to adress any potential issues, refine deisgns and art work to ensure they align with your business ethos and goals.

Digital Mock-ups & Drafts

Final drafts and Digital mock-ups will be delivered for approcal to build the prototypes ready for user testing

Prototyping & User Testing

After approval of final design briefs. Prototypes will be developed ready for field and user testing to ensure the project functions as expected.

Mission Deployed

The final build will be delivered in both soft and hardcopy. Deployment will commence on approval of working prototypes
